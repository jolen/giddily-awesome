Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. What is ZFT?

    We are a group of traders who believe that technical analysis is all you need to be profitable in trading. We do not need to hype, listen to tips and rumors, front run, learn fundamental analysis, and the art of speculation in order to be good at trading. What we see in our charts is enough data for us to make sound trading decisions.

    As for the origins of our club's name, some random people were spreading rumors that we were insiders and that we were group trading certain stocks and that's why we were able to gain so much from trading.

    "They bought at the lows? Balita ko maraming insiders jan. Sila mga jockey ng IS. Tribe ni Zeefreaks."

    And the rest was history.

  • 3. How much is the fee to join?

    There are no fixed fees. The fees vary from batch to batch and student to student. It can range from 25K to as high as 250K to accommodate different types of people. Once the Project Seed page announces the start of enrollment, just submit your requirements and we will inform you of your appropriate fees.

  • 4. Can OFWs or those with zero knowledge in trading join?

    YES. We actually prefer beginners or those with empty cups as they do not have old and bad habits that needs to be changed. But aside from that, we prefer students who can also walk the talk and spend actual TIME and EFFORT in learning.
