Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I be part of the tribe?

    Currently there's only one way to join ZFT, and that's via the Project Seed mentoring program. Interested applicants will be given tasks or challenges as part of their application requirements, it is critical to follow the instructions given, if you want to even be considered.

    To know when the next batch of applicants will be open, follow Zeefreaks Tribe on Facebook.

  • How much is the joining fee for Project Seed?

    The fees vary constantly. It is announced to the selected applicants, fees are flexible for different type of individual portfolio, our main qualification on joining ZFT is not on the size of your portfolio, we select applicants base on their drive and determination and the fees varies depending on their financial capabilities.

  • Can an OFW be part of the tribe?

    Definitely, we strive to help as many OFWs as we can, we rejoice every time one of our OFW tribe members are able to quit their job and come home for good to be a full time trader. We want more Filipinos succeeding in our own country.

  • Can a beginner join the tribe?

    Absolutely, we like working with beginners or those as they say it ‘with empty cups’. Aside from the level of your knowledge in trading, we prefer those that spend actual time and effort in learning.

  • Do you conduct seminars?

    We’d like to make sure that we will be able to deliver the best learning experience that ZFT has to offer. We are currently brewing something and it will be announced soon.
